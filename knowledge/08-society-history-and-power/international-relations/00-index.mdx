# 🌐 International Relations

## 📚 Overview

How do nations interact? What governs the relationships between states? International relations is the study of diplomacy, conflict, cooperation, and the institutions that manage them.

This shelf covers the major theories (realism, liberalism, constructivism), diplomatic history and foreign policy, and the international organizations that shape global governance.
