# 🚀 Science Fiction

## 📚 Overview

Science fiction is the literature of "what if?" It imagines futures shaped by technology, exploration, and the unexpected consequences of human choices. This shelf covers the major subgenres: hard science fiction, space opera, cyberpunk and dystopian futures, and AI-centered sci-fi.
