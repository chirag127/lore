# ⚙️ Operations & Supply Chain

## 📚 Overview

Operations is how organizations deliver value. This shelf covers the key frameworks: Eli Goldratt's Theory of Constraints, lean operations, and Atul Gawande's Checklist Manifesto — the idea that even experts need checklists to avoid errors in complex processes.

These books are essential for anyone who manages operations, supply chains, or complex processes.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Theory of constraints: identifying and managing bottlenecks
- Lean operations: eliminating waste and maximizing flow
- The checklist manifesto: how checklists prevent errors in complex processes
- Supply chain design and management

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **The Goal** — Eli Goldratt: A business novel about the theory of constraints and how to manage bottlenecks.
- **The Checklist Manifesto** — Atul Gawande: How the simple checklist can save lives and improve performance in complex fields.
- **Theory of Constraints and Lean Operations** — The principles of lean manufacturing and continuous improvement.
