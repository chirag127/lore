Tiny Habits distills over twenty years of Stanford research into the simplest possible behavior change system. BJ Fogg, the Stanford behavior scientist who founded the Persuasive Technology Lab, argues that the key to lasting habit formation is not willpower or motivation but design — specifically, designing behaviors so small they require no motivation at all. The B=MAP formula (Behavior = Motivation x Ability x Prompt) has become one of the most influential frameworks in the habit industry, and Fogg's emphasis on celebration as a neurological rewiring tool remains his most distinctive contribution.
