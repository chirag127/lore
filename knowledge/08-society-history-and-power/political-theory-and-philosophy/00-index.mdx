# 🏛️ Political Theory & Philosophy

## 📚 Overview

Political theory asks the deepest questions about power, justice, and the good society. This shelf covers the major traditions: classical political philosophy (Plato, Aristotle), Enlightenment and social contract theory (Locke, Rousseau, Hobbes), modern political philosophy (Rawls, Nozick), civil society and social movements, and the challenges of disinformation and media literacy.
