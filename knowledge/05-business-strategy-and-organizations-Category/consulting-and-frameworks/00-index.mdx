# 📋 Consulting & Frameworks

## 📚 Overview

Management consulting has produced some of the most widely used problem-solving frameworks in business. This shelf covers the tools and methodologies: McKinsey's structured problem-solving approach, the frameworks used in case interviews, and the broader consulting methodology.

Whether you're preparing for consulting interviews or just want to think more rigorously about business problems, these books provide the tools.
