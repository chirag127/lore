# 🎁 Product Management

## 📚 Overview

Product management is the discipline of discovering what customers need, defining what to build, and shipping it successfully. This shelf covers the key frameworks: Rob Fitzpatrick's The Mom Test for customer conversations, product discovery techniques, and Pip Coburn's change function.

These books are essential for anyone who builds products — from startup founders to product managers at scale.

- **Product Discovery and Customer Development** — The frameworks for discovering what customers actually need.
- **Change Function** — Pip Coburn: How to create demand for your product in a crowded market.
