# 🎭 Poetry & Drama

## 📚 Overview

Poetry is language at its most concentrated. This shelf covers both the practice and the theory of poetry: from Western poetry and Indian poetry to spoken word and slam poetry, from the theory and criticism of poetry to the art of poetic expression.
