# 🏃 Exercise & Fitness

## 📚 Overview

Human beings evolved to move — to run, climb, lift, and endure. Modern life has made us sedentary, and the consequences are dire. This shelf covers the science and culture of physical fitness, from Christopher McDougall's exploration of ultra-running to the broader science of human performance.
