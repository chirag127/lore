# ⏳ Longevity & Aging

## 📚 Overview

We are living longer — but are we living better? Longevity science asks how we can extend not just lifespan but healthspan: the number of years we live in good health. This shelf covers the biology of aging, evidence-based interventions for longer life, and the emerging science of longevity.
