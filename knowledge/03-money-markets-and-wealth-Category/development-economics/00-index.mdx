# 🌍 Development Economics

## 📚 Overview

Why are some countries rich and others poor? What drives economic growth? How can policy reduce poverty and inequality? Development economics tackles these fundamental questions about human prosperity.

This shelf covers the theory and evidence behind economic development — from growth models to the role of institutions, from foreign aid to trade policy.

## 🎯 Scope

| Included | Excluded |
|----------|----------|
| Economic growth theory | Macroeconomics (→ Macroeconomics) |
| Development policy | Microeconomics (→ Microeconomics) |
| Poverty and inequality | Political economy (→ Political Economy) |

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Macroeconomics** covers macroeconomic theory for developed economies.
- **Microeconomics** covers microeconomic theory.
- **Political Economy** covers the political dimensions of economic development.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Economic growth theory
- Development policy and institutions
- Poverty, inequality, and human development

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Economic development is the most powerful force for reducing human suffering. Understanding development economics helps us design better policies, allocate resources more effectively, and build a more prosperous world.
