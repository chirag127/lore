# 🎬 Film & Media Studies

## 📚 Overview

Film and media are the dominant art forms of the modern era. This shelf covers the full spectrum: from film history and cinema movements to film theory and critical methods, from documentary and non-fiction cinema to journalism and television studies.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Drama & Theatre** covers live performance.
- **Music** covers music in film.
- **Literary Criticism** covers the theory of media criticism.

This category focuses on **film and media** as art forms and cultural forces.

- **Film Theory and Critical Methods** — The frameworks for analyzing and understanding film.
- **Documentary and Non-Fiction Cinema** — The art of telling true stories on screen.
- **Film Production and Screenwriting** — The craft of making films.

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Film and media shape how we see the world. Understanding how they work helps you be a more critical consumer and a more effective creator of media content.
