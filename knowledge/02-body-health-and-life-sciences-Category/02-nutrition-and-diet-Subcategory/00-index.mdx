# 🥗 Nutrition & Diet

## 📚 Overview

You are what you eat — and the modern food system makes it hard to eat well. This shelf covers the science of nutrition, the politics of the food industry, and the evidence-based strategies for optimizing your diet.

From Michael Moss's exposé of the processed food industry to the biochemistry of metabolism, these books reveal what's really in your food and how it affects your body.

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Diet is one of the most powerful determinants of health — and one of the most manipulated by commercial interests. Understanding nutrition science gives you the power to make informed choices about what you put in your body.
