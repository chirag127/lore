# 📖 Literary Criticism

## 📚 Overview

How do we read and interpret literature? This shelf covers the major schools of literary criticism: formalism, structuralism, post-structuralism, Marxism, feminism, postcolonialism, ecocriticism, and more.

With over 20 books, this is one of the most comprehensive literary criticism collections in BookAtlas. It includes Terry Eagleton's accessible introductions, Peter Barry's Beginning Theory, the Norton Anthology of Theory and Criticism, and M.H. Abrams's The Mirror and the Lamp.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **After Theory** — Terry Eagleton: What comes after the great theory revolution in literary studies.
- **Beginning Theory** — Peter Barry: An accessible introduction to the major schools of literary and cultural theory.
- **The Mirror and the Lamp** — M.H. Abrams: A classic study of romantic theory and the critical tradition.
- **Theory of Literature** — Wellek & Warren: A systematic approach to the theory of literature.
- **Norton Anthology of Theory and Criticism** — The comprehensive anthology of critical texts from Plato to the present.
