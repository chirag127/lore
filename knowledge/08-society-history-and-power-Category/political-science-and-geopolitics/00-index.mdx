# 🏛️ Political Science & Geopolitics

## 📚 Overview

Political science is the systematic study of political institutions and behavior. This shelf covers the core topics: democratic systems and electoral theory, federalism and decentralization, political parties and party systems, and the comparison of presidential and parliamentary systems.

These books provide the framework for understanding how political systems work and why they differ.
