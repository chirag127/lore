# 🐉 Fantasy

## 📚 Overview

Fantasy is the literature of the impossible made real. This shelf covers the major subgenres: epic fantasy with its secondary worlds and grand quests, mythopoeic fantasy that draws on ancient myths and legends, and urban fantasy that brings the magical into the modern world.

## 🎯 Scope

| Included | Excluded |
|----------|----------|
| Epic fantasy and secondary world-building | Science fiction (→ Science Fiction) |
| Mythopoeic fantasy | Genre fiction (→ Genre Fiction) |
| Urban fantasy and contemporary fantasy | Literary criticism (→ Literary Criticism) |

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Science Fiction** covers science-based speculative fiction.
- **Genre Fiction** covers mythological retellings.
- **Literary Criticism** covers the analysis of fantasy literature.

This category focuses on **fantasy literature** — stories of magic, myth, and the impossible.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Epic fantasy: secondary world-building, quest narratives, and heroic fantasy
- Mythopoeic fantasy: drawing on ancient myths and legends
- Urban fantasy: magic in the modern world
