# The Oxford English Dictionary (Compact Edition)

## Executive Summary

The Oxford English Dictionary is the greatest dictionary ever created and the definitive record of the English language. The Compact Edition reproduces the complete text of the 20-volume second edition in a two-volume set using micrographic photography, requiring a magnifying glass to read. It contains over 600,000 words with their full history, etymology, and illustrative quotations spanning more than 1,000 years of English usage. It is not merely a dictionary but a history of the English language and the culture it expresses.



## Key Takeaways

- **The OED is the most comprehensive dictionary** of English ever created, with over 600,000 words
- **Each word entry traces** the complete history of usage through dated quotations
- **Etymology is traced** back through Proto-Indo-European where possible
- **Pronunciation is given** for British and American English
- **The Compact Edition** uses micrographic type requiring a magnifying glass

## Who Should Read

- **Word lovers**: The deepest possible dive into English vocabulary
- **Academic researchers**: The definitive record of English words
- **Writers and editors**: Understand the full history and nuance of vocabulary
- **Etymology enthusiasts**: Trace words back to their origins

## Who Should Skip

- Casual readers who just want **spelling and basic definitions**
- Those without **access to a magnifying glass**
- **Budget-conscious** buyers — the online subscription may be more practical

## Difficulty

**Hard** — Reference work requiring patience and a magnifying glass for the compact edition. Content is scholarly and dense.

## Reading Time

- **Full Read**: Not applicable — a reference work, not a linear read
- **Browsing**: Endless — a lifetime of exploration

## Final Verdict

**Essential** for serious word lovers and the definitive reference for the English language. The Compact Edition is an iconic object — beautiful, impractical, and awe-inspiring. For practical daily use, the online subscription is recommended, but owning the Compact Edition is a statement of devotion to the language.
