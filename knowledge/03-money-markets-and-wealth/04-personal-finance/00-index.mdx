# 💰 Personal Finance

## 📚 Overview

Personal finance is the most practical financial topic — it's about your money, your life, and your future. This shelf covers the full spectrum of personal finance: budgeting, saving, debt elimination, financial independence, and the psychology of money.

From Dave Ramsey's debt snowball to Ramit Sethi's automated wealth, from Robert Kiyosaki's rich dad to Thomas Stanley's millionaire next door, these books provide actionable strategies for taking control of your financial life.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Investment Theory** covers investment strategies and philosophy.
- **Finance** covers financial instruments and markets.
- **Behavioral Finance** covers the psychology of financial decision-making.

This category focuses on **your personal money** — earning, spending, saving, and building wealth.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Rich Dad Poor Dad** — Robert Kiyosaki: The classic that changed how millions think about money, assets, and financial education.
- **I Will Teach You to Be Rich** — Ramit Sethi: A no-guilt, no-excuses, 6-week program for taking control of your money.
- **The Total Money Makeover** — Dave Ramsey: A proven plan for financial fitness — get out of debt and build wealth.
- **The Millionaire Next Door** — Thomas Stanley: The surprising secrets of America's wealthy — they're not who you think.
- **Die with Zero** — Bill Perkins: Why you should spend your money before you die — and how to optimize your life experiences.

Personal finance is the foundation of financial well-being. No matter how much you earn, if you don't manage it well, you'll struggle. These books provide the practical strategies and mindset shifts you need to take control of your financial life.
