# 🎲 Probability, Forecasting & Risk

## 📚 Overview

The future is uncertain. Yet we must make decisions today that depend on what happens tomorrow. This shelf covers the science of reasoning under uncertainty — from Bayesian updating and expected value to superforecasting and tail risk.

These books teach you how to make better predictions, avoid catastrophic risks, and think clearly about an uncertain world. They include Philip Tetlock's groundbreaking superforecasting research and Nassim Taleb's work on black swans and antifragility.
