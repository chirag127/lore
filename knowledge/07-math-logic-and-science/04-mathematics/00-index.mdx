# 📐 Mathematics

## 📚 Overview

Mathematics is the language in which the universe is written. This shelf covers the major branches: abstract algebra, real and complex analysis, differential geometry, linear algebra, number theory, optimization, and the philosophy of mathematics.

These books provide the mathematical foundation for understanding everything from physics to finance, from computer science to philosophy.
