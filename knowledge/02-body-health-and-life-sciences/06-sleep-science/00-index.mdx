# 😴 Sleep Science

## 📚 Overview

Sleep is not a luxury — it's a biological necessity. Yet modern life treats it as optional. This shelf covers the science of sleep: why we need it, what happens in the brain during sleep, the consequences of sleep deprivation, and evidence-based strategies for sleeping better.

From Matthew Walker's groundbreaking research to Chris Winter's practical sleep solutions, these books will convince you to take sleep seriously.
