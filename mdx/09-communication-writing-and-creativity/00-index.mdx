# 09 - Communication Writing And Creativity

The tools of human expression - from writing craft and storytelling to rhetoric, public speaking, design, photography, music, and visual arts.

## Scope

This category covers books on the fundamental principles and practices of communication writing and creativity, including its subdisciplines, history, and modern applications.

## What Belongs Here

Books that provide foundational knowledge, practical skills, and deep understanding of communication writing and creativity - from introductory overviews to advanced specialized works.

## What Does NOT Belong Here

Books that primarily belong in other categories are not included here, even if they touch on related topics. Each book is placed in the category that best represents its core subject matter.
