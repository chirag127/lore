# 🧬 Biology & Life Sciences

## 📚 Overview

Biology is the study of life itself. From the molecular machinery inside every cell to the grand patterns of evolution, this shelf covers the fundamental science of living organisms.

This collection includes essential texts on molecular biology — the processes that govern how cells function, replicate, and communicate.

## 🎯 Scope

| Included | Excluded |
|----------|----------|
| Molecular and cell biology | Evolutionary biology (→ Biology & Evolution) |
| Genetics and genomics | Medicine (→ Medicine & Clinical Science) |
| Biochemistry | Exercise science (→ Exercise & Fitness) |

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Medicine & Clinical Science** covers the application of biology to human health.
- **Biology & Evolution** covers evolutionary biology and natural selection.
- **Neuroscience** (in Math, Logic & Science) covers the biology of the nervous system.

This category focuses on the **molecular and cellular foundations** of life.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Molecular biology of the cell
- Cellular processes and signaling
- DNA, RNA, and protein synthesis

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Molecular Biology of the Cell** — Alberts: The definitive textbook on how cells work, from the molecules that make them up to the processes that keep them alive.

## 🗺 Related Categories

- **Biology & Evolution** — Evolutionary biology and natural selection
- **Medicine & Clinical Science** — Clinical applications of biology
- **Neuroscience** — The biology of the brain and nervous system

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Molecular biology is the foundation of modern medicine, biotechnology, and our understanding of life itself. Understanding how cells work at the molecular level is essential for anyone interested in health, medicine, or the science of life.
