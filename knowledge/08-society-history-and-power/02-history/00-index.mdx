# 📜 History

## 📚 Overview

History is the story of who we are and how we got here. This shelf covers major historical topics: from the ancient world and the Cold War to the digital revolution and the innovators who built the modern era.

These books span millennia and continents, providing the context for understanding the present.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **World History & Civilizations** covers the history of specific civilizations.
- **Political Theory & Philosophy** covers political thought.
- **History & Philosophy of Science** covers the history of science.

This category focuses on **major historical events and themes** that shaped the modern world.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **The History of the Ancient World** — Susan Wise Bauer: A narrative history from the earliest accounts to the fall of Rome.
- **The Innovators** — Walter Isaacson: How the collaborative geniuses of the digital revolution created the computer and the internet.
- **Homo Deus** — Yuval Noah Harari: A brief history of tomorrow — where is humanity headed?
- **Cold War and Superpower Competition** — The defining conflict of the second half of the 20th century.
- **History of the United States** — The story of America from colony to superpower.

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

History is the laboratory of human experience. Understanding the past helps us make sense of the present and avoid repeating the mistakes of those who came before.
