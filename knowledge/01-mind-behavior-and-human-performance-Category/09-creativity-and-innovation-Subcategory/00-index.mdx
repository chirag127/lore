# 💡 Creativity & Innovation

## 📚 Overview

Creativity isn't a mystical gift reserved for artists and inventors. It's a cognitive process that can be understood, practiced, and cultivated. This shelf explores how new ideas emerge, what blocks creative thinking, and how to build a sustainable creative practice.

From design thinking to the psychology of originality, from overcoming creative blocks to understanding the creative personality, these books provide both the theory and the practice of innovation.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Business Strategy** covers innovation management and organizational creativity.
- **Art & Design** (in Fiction & Literature) covers the practice of art and design.
- **Writing Craft** covers creative writing specifically.

This category focuses on the **psychology and process of creativity** — how ideas form and how to cultivate them.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- The creative process and its stages
- Overcoming creative blocks and resistance
- Design thinking methodology
- Originality, taste, and the creative personality
- Turning ideas into action

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **The War of Art** — Steven Pressfield: A battle cry against the inner resistance that keeps us from doing our best work.
- **Creative Process and Overcoming Creative Blocks** — Understanding the psychology of creative work.
- **Design Thinking and Creative Problem-Solving** — A structured approach to innovation.
- **Originality, Taste, and the Creative Personality** — What makes some people more creative than others.

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

In a world of increasing automation, creativity is the ultimate competitive advantage. These books teach you how to generate novel ideas, overcome the resistance that blocks creative work, and build a sustainable practice of innovation.
