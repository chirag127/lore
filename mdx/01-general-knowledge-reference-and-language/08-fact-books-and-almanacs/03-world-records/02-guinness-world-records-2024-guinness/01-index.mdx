# Guinness World Records 2024

## Executive Summary

Guinness World Records is the best-selling annual book of superlatives, documenting the most extraordinary achievements across every field of human and natural endeavor. The 2024 edition features thousands of new and updated records, verified by official adjudicators, covering everything from sports and science to technology and the natural world.



## Key Takeaways

- **Documents thousands** of records across all categories
- **Records require** official verification and evidence
- **Many records** are broken each year
- **Coverage includes** human achievement, natural world, technology
- **Inspires readers** to push beyond perceived limits
