# 🤖 Artificial Intelligence

## 📚 Overview

Artificial intelligence is transforming every aspect of human life. This shelf covers the full spectrum of AI — from the mathematical foundations of machine learning to the engineering of large language models, from computer vision to reinforcement learning, from AI ethics to governance frameworks.

With over 40 books, this is one of the most comprehensive collections in BookAtlas. It includes Ian Goodfellow's Deep Learning, François Chollet's Deep Learning with Python, Sebastian Raschka's Build a Large Language Model from Scratch, and many more essential texts.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Software Engineering** covers the engineering practices for building AI systems.
- **Data & Algorithms** covers the data structures and algorithms used in AI.
- **System Design & Architecture** covers the infrastructure for deploying AI at scale.

This category focuses on the **algorithms, models, and theory** of artificial intelligence.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Deep learning: feedforward networks, backpropagation, CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs
- Transformers and attention mechanisms
- Large language models and prompt engineering
- Natural language processing and classical NLP
- Computer vision: image recognition, object detection, segmentation
- Reinforcement learning: MDPs, policy gradients, model-based RL
- Generative models
- AI ethics, governance, and value alignment
- Model deployment, serving, and monitoring
- Multimodal vision-language models
- Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Deep Learning** — Ian Goodfellow: The definitive textbook on deep learning by three of the field's leading researchers.
- **Deep Learning with Python** — François Chollet: A practical, hands-on introduction to deep learning using Keras.
- **Build a Large Language Model from Scratch** — Sebastian Raschka: How LLMs work, from tokenization to training.
- **Hands-On Machine Learning** — Aurélien Géron: The most practical introduction to machine learning with scikit-learn and TensorFlow.
- **Designing Machine Learning Systems** — Chip Huyen: How to build production-ready ML systems, not just models.
