# 🌍 World History & Civilizations

## 📚 Overview

World history is the grand narrative of human civilization. This shelf covers the major themes: from prehistory and ancient civilizations to the rise and fall of the Third Reich, from the Silk Roads to the weirdest people in the world, from Sapiens to the Dawn of Everything.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Sapiens** — Yuval Noah Harari: A narrative history of humankind from the Stone Age to the present.
- **Guns, Germs, and Steel** — Jared Diamond: Why some civilizations conquered others — and why geography matters more than genetics.
- **The Silk Roads** — Peter Frankopan: A new history of the world centered on the crossroads of civilization.
- **The Lessons of History** — Will Durant: What 5,000 years of human history teach us about the future.
- **World War II and the Holocaust** — The defining catastrophe of the 20th century.

Understanding the sweep of human history gives you perspective. It helps you see patterns, appreciate diversity, and understand how the present emerged from the past. These books tell the story of us — all of us.
