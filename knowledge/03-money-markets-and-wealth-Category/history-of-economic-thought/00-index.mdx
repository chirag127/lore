# 📜 History of Economic Thought

## 📚 Overview

Economic ideas shape the world. From Adam Smith's invisible hand to Keynes's demand management, from Marx's critique of capitalism to Friedman's monetarism, the history of economic thought is the history of how we've tried to understand and manage prosperity.

This shelf covers the major schools of economic thought and the thinkers who shaped them. Understanding where economic ideas came from helps you evaluate them critically and think more clearly about economic policy.

- Classical economics: Smith, Ricardo, Mill, Marx

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Economic ideas have consequences. The policies that shape our world — from taxation to trade, from monetary policy to regulation — are rooted in economic theories. Understanding the history of these ideas helps you think more critically about the economic debates of today.
