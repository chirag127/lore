# 🏢 Industrial-Organizational Psychology

## 📚 Overview

Industrial-organizational (I/O) psychology applies psychological science to the workplace. How do you select the right people? How do you motivate teams? How do you build organizations that bring out the best in people?

This shelf covers the evidence-based principles behind effective leadership, high-performing teams, and workplace motivation. It's essential reading for anyone who manages people or wants to understand organizational behavior.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Leadership** (in Business) covers leadership from a management perspective.
- **Management & Organizational Behavior** (in Business) covers organizational design and culture.
- **Social Psychology** covers group dynamics in general, not specifically workplace contexts.

This category focuses on the **psychological science** of workplace behavior — selection, motivation, leadership, and team performance.

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

We spend most of our waking lives at work. Understanding the psychology of the workplace — what motivates people, how teams function, what makes leaders effective — is essential for anyone who wants to build a career, lead a team, or create a great organization.
