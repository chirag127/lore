# ⚛️ Physics

## 📚 Overview

Physics is the most fundamental science. It describes the laws that govern everything from subatomic particles to the largest structures in the universe. This shelf covers the major branches: classical mechanics and thermodynamics, electromagnetism and optics, quantum mechanics and quantum field theory, relativity, particle physics, and condensed matter physics.

These books provide the foundation for understanding how the universe works at its most fundamental level.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Classical mechanics and thermodynamics: motion, energy, and heat
- Electromagnetism and optics: light, electricity, and magnetism
- Quantum mechanics and quantum field theory: the strange world of the very small
- Relativity: special and general — space, time, and gravity
- Particle physics and the standard model: the fundamental particles and forces
- Condensed matter physics: the physics of solids and liquids
