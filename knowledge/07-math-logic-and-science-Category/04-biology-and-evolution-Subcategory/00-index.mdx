# 🧬 Biology & Evolution

## 📚 Overview

Evolution is the unifying principle of biology. This shelf covers the major works on evolutionary biology and genetics: from Darwin's Origin of Species to Richard Dawkins's The Selfish Gene and The Extended Phenotype, from Siddhartha Mukherjee's The Gene to Michael Pollan's The Botany of Desire.

These books tell the story of life on Earth — how it began, how it diversified, and how natural selection shapes every living thing.

- Evolutionary biology and natural selection

- The Botany of Desire: how plants evolved to satisfy human desires

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **The Origin of Species** — Charles Darwin: The book that changed our understanding of life on Earth.
- **The Selfish Gene** — Richard Dawkins: The gene-centered view of evolution that transformed biology.
- **The Gene** — Siddhartha Mukherjee: A sweeping history of the gene — from Mendel to CRISPR.
- **The Extended Phenotype** — Richard Dawkins: How genes shape the world beyond the organism.
- **The Botany of Desire** — Michael Pollan: How apples, tulips, marijuana, and potatoes evolved to satisfy human desires.
