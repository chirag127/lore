# 🤝 Sales & Business Development

## 📚 Overview

Sales is the engine of revenue. This shelf covers the key topics: consultative selling, enterprise B2B sales, and the sales funnel and CRM systems that support them.

Whether you're a founder selling your first product or a sales leader building a team, these books provide the frameworks for selling effectively.
