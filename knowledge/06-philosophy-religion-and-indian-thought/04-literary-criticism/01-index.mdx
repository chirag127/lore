# 📖 Literary Criticism

## 📚 Overview

How do we read and interpret literature? What frameworks help us understand what texts mean? Literary criticism provides the tools for analyzing, interpreting, and evaluating literature.

This shelf covers the major approaches: formalism, structuralism, post-structuralism, Marxism, feminism, postcolonialism, and more. It includes Terry Eagleton's accessible introductions, Peter Barry's Beginning Theory, and the Norton Anthology of Theory and Criticism.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Beginning Theory** — Peter Barry: An accessible introduction to the major schools of literary and cultural theory.
- **Literary Theory** — Terry Eagleton: A concise, witty overview of the major movements in literary theory.
- **The Mirror and the Lamp** — M.H. Abrams: A classic study of romantic theory and the critical tradition.
- **Theory of Literature** — Wellek & Warren: A systematic approach to the theory of literature.
- **The Norton Anthology of Theory and Criticism** — The comprehensive anthology of critical texts from Plato to the present.
