# 03 - Money Markets And Wealth

The principles of finance and investing - from personal wealth building and behavioral finance to value investing, risk management, quantitative trading, and market history.

## Scope

This category covers books on the fundamental principles and practices of money markets and wealth, including its subdisciplines, history, and modern applications.

## What Belongs Here

## What Does NOT Belong Here
