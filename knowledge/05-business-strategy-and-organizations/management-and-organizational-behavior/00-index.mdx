# 🏢 Management & Organizational Behavior

## 📚 Overview

Management is the practice of getting things done through people. This shelf covers the core topics: people management, performance reviews, and the dynamics of organizational culture and behavior.

While leadership inspires, management executes. These books provide the practical tools for building and running effective organizations.
