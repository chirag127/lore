# 📣 Marketing & Brand Strategy

## 📚 Overview

Marketing is how businesses create, communicate, and deliver value to customers. This shelf covers the full spectrum: from Al Ries's positioning to David Ogilvy's advertising wisdom, from Geoffrey Moore's Crossing the Chasm to Donald Miller's StoryBrand, from content marketing to paid acquisition.

With over 15 books, this is one of the most comprehensive marketing collections in BookAtlas. It includes classics like Ogilvy on Advertising, Scientific Advertising, and Made to Stick alongside modern frameworks like Building a StoryBrand and Traction.
