# 🏘️ Sociology & Anthropology

## 📚 Overview

Sociology and anthropology study human societies and cultures from the inside out. This shelf covers the major topics: cultural anthropology and ethnography, physical and evolutionary anthropology, social anthropology and kinship, and the classic sociological texts by Durkheim and Weber.

These books provide the frameworks for understanding how human societies are organized, how cultures differ and converge, and how social structures shape individual lives.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **The Division of Labor in Society** — Émile Durkheim: A foundational text of sociology on how specialization binds societies together.
- **The Protestant Ethic and the Spirit of Capitalism** — Max Weber: How religious values shaped the rise of capitalism.
- **Cultural Anthropology and Ethnography** — The methods and findings of cultural anthropology.
- **Social Anthropology and Kinship** — How kinship systems organize human societies.
- **Physical and Evolutionary Anthropology** — The biological basis of human social behavior.
