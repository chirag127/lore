# 11 - Reference And General Knowledge

The essential reference works - from encyclopedias and dictionaries to atlases, biographical references, fact books, and general knowledge handbooks.

## Scope

This category covers books on the fundamental principles and practices of reference and general knowledge, including its subdisciplines, history, and modern applications.

## What Belongs Here

Books that provide foundational knowledge, practical skills, and deep understanding of reference and general knowledge - from introductory overviews to advanced specialized works.

## What Does NOT Belong Here

Books that primarily belong in other categories are not included here, even if they touch on related topics. Each book is placed in the category that best represents its core subject matter.
