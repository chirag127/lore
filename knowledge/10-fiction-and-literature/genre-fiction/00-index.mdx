# 📚 Genre Fiction

## 📚 Overview

Every culture has its stories — the myths, legends, and epics that define its identity and transmit its values. This shelf covers the great mythological traditions: Greek and Roman mythology, Indian epics, and Norse, Celtic, and world mythology.

These are the stories that have shaped human civilization.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Literary Criticism** covers the analysis of mythological texts.
- **Fantasy** covers modern mythological fiction.
- **Drama & Theatre** covers mythological drama.

This category focuses on the **mythological traditions** that underpin world literature.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Greek and Roman mythology: the gods, heroes, and stories of the ancient world
- Indian epics: the Mahabharata, Ramayana, and the great stories of India
- Norse, Celtic, and world mythology: the stories of cultures around the globe
