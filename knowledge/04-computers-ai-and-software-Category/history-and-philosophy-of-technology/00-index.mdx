# 🏛️ History & Philosophy of Technology

## 📚 Overview

Technology is not neutral. It shapes how we live, work, think, and relate to each other — and it is shaped by the societies that create it. This shelf covers the history and philosophy of technology: from the industrial revolutions that transformed the world to the philosophical questions raised by AI and human-machine relations.

These books help you think critically about technology — not just as a tool, but as a force that reshapes human civilization.

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Technology is reshaping every aspect of human life. Understanding its history and philosophy helps you think critically about the choices we make — as individuals, organizations, and societies — about which technologies to build and how to use them.
