Jim Kwik, a renowned memory coach who has trained celebrities, executives, and students, presents a comprehensive system for unlocking cognitive potential. The book is organized around three pillars: Mindset (beliefs about your abilities), Motivation (purpose and drive), and Methods (specific techniques for reading, memory, focus, and thinking).

*Limitless* is a practical toolkit rather than a scientific treatise. Kwik draws on his personal story—he suffered a traumatic brain injury as a child and was labeled "the boy with the broken brain"—to illustrate that cognitive ability can be developed. The book provides concrete techniques for speed reading, memorizing names and numbers, entering flow states, and overcoming the mental fog of digital distraction.
