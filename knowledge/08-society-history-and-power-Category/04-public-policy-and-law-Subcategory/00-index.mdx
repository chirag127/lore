# ⚖️ Public Policy & Law

## 📚 Overview

Public policy is how governments translate values into action. This shelf covers the major topics: policy design, implementation, and evaluation; regulatory theory and administrative law; international law; and foundational legal texts like H.L.A. Hart's The Concept of Law, Oliver Wendell Holmes's The Common Law, and B.R. Ambedkar's The Constitution of India.

These books provide the framework for understanding how laws are made, interpreted, and enforced.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **The Concept of Law** — H.L.A. Hart: The most important work of legal philosophy in the 20th century.
- **The Common Law** — Oliver Wendell Holmes: A classic introduction to the American legal system.
- **The Constitution of India** — B.R. Ambedkar: The foundational document of the world's largest democracy.
- **Policy Design, Implementation, Evaluation** — How to design policies that actually work.
- **International Law** — Malcolm Shaw: A comprehensive introduction to the law governing relations among states.
