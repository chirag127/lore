# ⚗️ Chemistry

## 📚 Overview

Chemistry is the central science — it connects physics to biology, materials to medicine, the atomic to the macroscopic. This shelf covers the major branches: inorganic and organic chemistry, physical chemistry and thermochemistry.

These books provide the foundation for understanding how matter behaves, reacts, and transforms.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Biology & Evolution** covers biochemistry and molecular biology.
- **Engineering & Technology** covers materials science and chemical engineering.
- **Physics** covers the physical principles underlying chemistry.

This category focuses on the **science of matter and its transformations**.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Inorganic chemistry: elements, compounds, and reactions
- Organic chemistry: carbon-based molecules and life's chemistry
- Physical chemistry and thermochemistry: energy, entropy, and chemical equilibrium

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Inorganic and Organic Chemistry** — The foundational principles of chemistry, from elements to complex molecules.
- **Physical Chemistry and Thermochemistry** — The energy transformations that drive chemical reactions.

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Chemistry is the science of stuff — what things are made of and how they change. Understanding chemistry gives you insight into everything from cooking to medicine, from materials to the environment.
