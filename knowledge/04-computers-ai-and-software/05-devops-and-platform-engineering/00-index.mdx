# ⚙️ DevOps & Platform Engineering

## 📚 Overview

DevOps is the cultural and technical movement that bridges the gap between development and operations. This shelf covers the key practices: continuous integration and deployment, container orchestration, infrastructure as code, site reliability engineering, and observability.

From Nicole Forsgren's Accelerate to Google's SRE books, from Michael Nygard's Release It to the practices of monitoring and incident management, these books provide the foundation for delivering software reliably at scale.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Accelerate** — Nicole Forsgren: The science of DevOps — what makes high-performing engineering teams.
- **Site Reliability Engineering** — Betsy Beyer: Google's guide to running production systems reliably.
- **Release It** — Michael Nygard: Design and deploy production-ready software that doesn't fail catastrophically.
- **CI/CD** — Building pipelines that automate testing and deployment.
- **Container Orchestration** — Managing containers at scale with Kubernetes.
