# 🇮🇳 Indian Finance

## 📚 Overview

India's financial system is one of the most dynamic and complex in the world. From the Bombay Stock Exchange to the Reserve Bank of India, from the rise of digital payments to the challenges of financial inclusion, Indian finance is a story of rapid transformation.

This shelf covers the Indian financial landscape — equity market investing, the regulatory framework, and the unique characteristics of India's financial system.
