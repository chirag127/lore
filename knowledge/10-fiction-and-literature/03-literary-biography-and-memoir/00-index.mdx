# 📖 Literary Biography & Memoir

## 📚 Overview

What makes a great writer, artist, or thinker? This shelf covers the lives of creative people: from Walter Isaacson's biographies of Einstein and Steve Jobs to Patti Smith's Just Kids, from Nelson Mandela's Long Walk to Freedom to Richard Feynman's Surely You're Joking.

These are the stories of people who created, thought, and lived at the highest level.

## 🎯 Scope

| Included | Excluded |
|----------|----------|

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Historical Biography & Memoir** covers political and social leaders.
- **Literary Fiction** covers fictional narratives.

This category focuses on the **lives of creative people**.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Einstein: his life and universe
- Steve Jobs: the biography
- Just Kids: Patti Smith's memoir of her relationship with Robert Mapplethorpe
- Long Walk to Freedom: Nelson Mandela's autobiography
- Surely You're Joking, Mr. Feynman: the adventures of a curious character
- Becoming: Michelle Obama's memoir

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Einstein: His Life and Universe** — Walter Isaacson: The definitive biography of the 20th century's greatest scientist.
- **Steve Jobs** — Walter Isaacson: The biography of the Apple co-founder who changed the world.
- **Just Kids** — Patti Smith: A memoir of love, art, and friendship in 1970s New York.
- **Long Walk to Freedom** — Nelson Mandela: The autobiography of one of history's greatest leaders.
- **Surely You're Joking, Mr. Feynman** — Richard Feynman: The adventures of a brilliant, irreverent physicist.

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

The lives of creative people inspire us to pursue our own creative paths. These stories show what's possible when talent meets dedication, and they reveal the human beings behind the work we admire.
